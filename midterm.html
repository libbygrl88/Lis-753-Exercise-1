    ''Introduction''

    Welcome to MY Mill. We are a family owned farm and orchard in beautiful Romeo, Michigan. We have been serving the community for generation and hopeful for many more generations to come! Here you will find information on our location and opens hours(please note that open hours only apply to the weekends!). We have also added a glossary of apple and apple recipes for everyones enjoyment. Welcome and happy picking! Address: 6350 37 Mile Rd. Romeo, Mi 48065. Phone: (810) 798-3611 Open Hours: Sept 7th thru Nov 3rd 11am to 6pm P

    Apple picking is a time honored tradition at HY Cider Mill.Every year during the fall families come all over to spend time together and collect delicious apples. The following is a schedule for when the Orchard is open for apple picking and the apples available at that time.
