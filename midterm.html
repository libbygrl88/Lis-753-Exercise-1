''Guide to MY Mill''

''Introduction''

Welcome to MY Mill. We are a family owned farm and orchard in beautiful Romeo, Michigan. We have been serving the community of Romeo, Michigan for generation, and hopeful for many more generations to come!Although we are well known for the cider produced from our mill we also allow the public access to the ochards for a variety of activities. Visitors also love to end their visits with to our store where they buy all kinds of apple flavored treats from donuts to of course our famour cider. Here you will find information on our location and opens hours(please note that open hours only apply to the weekends!). We have also added a glossary of apple and apple recipes for everyones enjoyment. Welcome and happy picking!

Address: 6350 37 Mile Rd. Romeo, Mi 48065. Phone: (810) 798-3611 Open Hours: Sept 7th thru Nov 3rd 11am to 6pm
