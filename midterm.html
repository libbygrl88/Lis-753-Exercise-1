''Introduction to MY Mill''

Welcome to MY Mill. We are a family owned farm and orchard in beautiful Romeo, Michigan. We have been serving the community for generation and hopeful for many more generations to come!

Apple picking is a time honored tradition at HY Cider Mill.Every year during the fall families come all over to spend time together and collect delicious apples. The following is a schedule for when the Orchard is open for apple picking and the apples available at that time. Also please note that Honey Crisp Apples are available in store only beginning Sept 14th.

Sept 7th Macintosh and Gala
